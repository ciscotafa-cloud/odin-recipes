Lasagna


Description


Lasagna is a hearty, layered pasta dish known for its rich, comforting flavors and satisfying texture. It’s made by stacking wide, flat pasta sheets with layers of savory fillings—most commonly a slow-cooked meat sauce, creamy cheese (like ricotta and mozzarella), and a tomato-based sauce seasoned with herbs such as basil and oregano.


Ingridients


  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Prepare the meat sauce
  4. Combine pasta and guanciale/li>
  5. Make the ricotta mixture
  6. Start layering
  7. Bake
