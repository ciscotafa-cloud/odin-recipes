Spaghetti Carbonara


Description


Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish originating from Rome, known for its rich, creamy texture and savory flavor. The dish combines al dente spaghetti with a smooth sauce made from eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper. The sauce is gently cooked by the heat of the pasta, creating a silky, almost creamy consistency without any cream being used.


Ingridients


  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Prepare the guanciale
  3. Mix the sauce base
  4. Combine pasta and guanciale
  5. Add the egg mixture
  6. Adjust the texture
  7. Serve immediately
