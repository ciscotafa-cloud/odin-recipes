Hamburger


Description


A hamburger is a popular, handheld sandwich made with a cooked ground meat patty—usually beef—served inside a soft bun. The patty is typically grilled or pan-fried, giving it a juicy interior and a slightly crisp, flavorful exterior.


Ingridients


  • Ground beef
  • Sliced cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Pickles
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Cook the patty
  3. Assemble the burger
